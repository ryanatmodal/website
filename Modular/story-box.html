Our Story: 8 Years of Research with Transit Agencies

Modal was born from 8 years of cutting-edge research at Georgia Tech and the University of Michigan to solve a single problem: how to make on-demand transit affordable for transit agencies. Together with agency experts, our researchers developed the algorithms and optimization techniques that power modern on-demand transit.


We worked side-by-side with transit professionals to learn, improve, and test our technology across $5M in pilots in real communities. These partnerships inspired us to found Modal, to bring affordable on-demand to every city.
