üöá Multimodal, not Microtransit

Multimodal transportation integrates multiple forms of transit‚Äîbuses, trains, ride-sharing, bikes, and more‚Äîinto a seamless network. Unlike microtransit, which focuses solely on small-vehicle on-demand service, multimodal solutions provide riders with comprehensive journey planning that combines the best of fixed-route reliability with on-demand flexibility. This approach maximizes coverage, minimizes costs, and provides riders with more options to get where they need to go.
